SoWashCo Computer Camp

This summer, we are thrilled to be running SoWashCo Computer Camp for a second year!

Our computer summer camp is a hands-on and lecture-based program for elementary and middle school students who are curious about technology, programming, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and the nitty-gritty details about how computers function. No prior experience is required: just a willingness to learn and explore!
